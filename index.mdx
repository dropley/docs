# Dropley Documentation

Welcome to the Dropley documentation. Dropley is the easiest way on earth to publish anything generated by software — upload a ZIP, get a public URL, share in seconds. No account, no dashboard.

## Getting Started

New to Dropley? Start with the [Getting Started guide](/knowledge/getting-started) to publish your first artifact.

<Callout type="info">
  Dropley is completely free and requires no account. Upload a ZIP and share the URL — it's that simple.
</Callout>

## What is Dropley?

Dropley turns ZIP files into publicly accessible websites. Upload a ZIP containing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and assets, and Dropley serves them at a permanent URL. Choose how long your artifact lives — from 1 day to 30 days — and share the link with anyone.

### Key Features

- **One action**: Upload a ZIP. Get a URL. Done.
- **No account required**: No signup, no login, no dashboard.
- **Permanent URLs**: Your artifact lives at `dropley.app/p/{shortId}`.
- **Configurable expiration**: Choose 1, 3, 7, 15, or 30 days.
- **Two project types**: Single HTML pages or full ZIP projects with assets.
